CROSS-over
==========

Book
----

CROSS-over, Kunst, media en technologie in Vlaanderen.

Published by BAM / Lannoo and designed by OSP

This book received a Plantin-Moretus prize 2009 for best designed book in the
category non fiction. The jury about Cross-over: “It was love at first sight“.
The book, published by BAM and Lannoo and edited by Liesbeth Huybregts, uses
Libre Fonts and is produced with Scribus and many other F/LOSS tools
